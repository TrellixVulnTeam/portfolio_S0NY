About me

Hi, I am a web designer and aspiring developer based in Namibia. I am passionate about design, technology, entrepreneurship, health and fitness, and I love creating things. I believe that contrast makes life interesting; the juxtaposition of old-school and modern, natural and man-made, emotion and logic. There is beauty in finding a way in which two extreme opposites can not only co-exist, but also compliment one another. I enjoy challenges and opportunities for continued growth and learning. Almost anything is possible with the right amount of hunger, vision, work ethic and discipline. I am a bit of a perfectionist; true beauty can be found in the smallest detail.

My design process

Brief

The first thing that I do is define the problem that needs to be solved, and set out the goals for the project.

Precedent

I do research on similar projects completed by others, to help shape the vision and ideas for the project.

Concept

This is the stage that refines the thinking behind the design. I match the design ideas to the goals of the project.

Design

Where the magic happens. Finally, I use my tools and knowledge to turn concept into reality.
